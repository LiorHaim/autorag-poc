## Promotion & Band Movement

When an employee is promoted, their new salary is set at or above the minimum of the new level's band. If their current salary already exceeds the new band's minimum, they receive a promotion increase of at least 10%. Promotions into a new band also trigger an equity refresh grant at the midpoint of the new level's equity range.

## Salary Band Review Cadence

Salary bands are reviewed annually in October and updated effective January 1. The review considers:

| Factor | Data Source | Weight |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Market movement | Radford, Levels.fyi, Pave surveys | 40% |
| Competitive landscape | Offer data from recruiting pipeline | 25% |
| Internal equity | Compa-ratio analysis across demographics | 20% |
| Retention risk | Regretted attrition by level/team | 15% |

Employees are notified of any band changes during the January compensation cycle. If a band moves such that an employee falls below the new minimum, their salary is adjusted to the new minimum effective January 1.
