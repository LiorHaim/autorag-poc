# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## PTO Allocation

NovaTech Solutions provides the following annual PTO allocation based on tenure:

- **0-2 years**: 20 days per year (accrued at 1.67 days/month)
- **3-5 years**: 25 days per year (accrued at 2.08 days/month)
- **6-10 years**: 28 days per year (accrued at 2.33 days/month)
- **10+ years**: 32 days per year (accrued at 2.67 days/month)

## PTO Carryover

Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused PTO days into the following calendar year. Carried-over days must be used by March 31 of the new year or they will be forfeited. There is no payout for unused PTO except upon termination.

## Requesting PTO

- Requests of 1-3 days: Submit at least 1 week in advance
- Requests of 4-10 days: Submit at least 3 weeks in advance
- Requests of 10+ days: Submit at least 6 weeks in advance

All PTO requests are submitted through the HR portal (hr.novatech.internal) and require manager approval. Managers must respond within 3 business days.

## Holidays

NovaTech observes 11 paid holidays per year:
New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day.

Employees who are required to work on a company holiday receive 1.5x their normal pay rate plus a floating holiday to be used within 90 days.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 sick days per year. Sick leave does not accrue or carry over. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Bereavement Leave

NovaTech provides paid bereavement leave for employees who experience the loss of a family member:

- **Immediate family** (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days of paid leave
- **Extended family** (grandparent, grandchild, in-law, aunt/uncle): 3 days of paid leave
- **Close friend or colleague**: 1 day of paid leave

Bereavement leave does not count against PTO. Additional time off can be arranged through the employee's manager and HR on a case-by-case basis. Employees may also use PTO or unpaid leave to extend their bereavement period.

## Jury Duty and Civic Leave

Employees called for jury duty will receive their full regular pay for up to 10 business days per calendar year. If jury duty extends beyond 10 days, employees receive the difference between their regular salary and any jury duty compensation paid by the court. Employees must provide a copy of the jury summons to HR as soon as it is received.

Employees may also take up to 4 hours of paid time off to vote in federal, state, or local elections if their work schedule does not provide sufficient time outside working hours.

## Military Leave

NovaTech complies with the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA). Employees who are members of the uniformed services are entitled to:

- Up to 5 years of cumulative military leave with reemployment rights
- Continued health benefits for up to 24 months during military service (employee pays up to 102% of the full premium after 31 days)
- Salary differential pay: NovaTech pays the difference between military pay and regular salary for the first 12 months of active duty
- Return to the same or comparable position upon completion of service
- No loss of seniority, status, or pay rate

## Sabbatical

Employees with 7 or more years of continuous service at NovaTech are eligible for a paid sabbatical:

- **Duration**: 4 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Frequency**: Available once every 7 years of continuous service
- **Eligibility**: Must have a performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher for the last 2 review cycles
- **Planning**: Sabbatical requests must be submitted at least 3 months in advance and approved by the employee's VP
- **Coverage**: A coverage plan must be established before the sabbatical begins

Sabbatical time is separate from PTO and does not count against the annual PTO allocation.

## Volunteer Time Off (VTO)

NovaTech encourages community involvement. All full-time employees receive 16 hours (2 days) of paid Volunteer Time Off per year to participate in charitable or community service activities. VTO can be used in half-day or full-day increments. Volunteer activities must be with registered nonprofit organizations. Requests are submitted through the HR portal and do not require manager approval.
