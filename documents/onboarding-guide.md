# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## First Day

- Arrive at 9:00 AM and check in with reception
- Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned 1 week before start date)
- Collect your equipment from IT (laptop, badge, peripherals)
- Complete mandatory paperwork: I-9, W-4, direct deposit, emergency contacts
- Attend Welcome session with HR (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- Lunch with your team (company-sponsored)
- Set up workstation, email, Slack, and VPN access
- Review and sign the Employee Handbook acknowledgment

## First Week

- Attend "NovaTech 101" company orientation (Tuesday, full day)
- Complete mandatory compliance training modules:
  - Information Security Awareness (45 min)
  - Anti-Harassment Training (60 min)
  - Data Privacy & GDPR (30 min)
  - Code of Conduct (30 min)
- Meet with your manager to review 30/60/90 day expectations
- Schedule 1:1 introductions with key stakeholders
- Join relevant Slack channels and distribution lists

## First Month

- Complete role-specific technical onboarding
- Attend at least 2 "Coffee Chat" sessions with employees from other teams
- Set up your OKRs with your manager
- Complete all required compliance training (deadline: Day 30)
- Attend first team sprint/planning meeting
- Schedule your 30-day check-in with your manager

## Systems Access

You will need access to the following systems (request through IT portal):
- **Email & Calendar**: Microsoft 365
- **Communication**: Slack (primary), Microsoft Teams (external meetings)
- **Code**: GitHub Enterprise
- **Project Management**: Jira
- **Documentation**: Confluence
- **HR**: Workday
- **Expenses**: SAP Concur
- **Travel**: Navan
- **Learning**: LinkedIn Learning + internal LMS

## Your Onboarding Buddy

Every new employee is assigned an onboarding buddy from outside their immediate team. Your buddy will:
- Check in with you daily during week 1
- Answer informal questions about culture and norms
- Introduce you to people across the organization
- Be available for the first 90 days
