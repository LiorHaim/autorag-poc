# Benefits Overview

## Health Insurance

NovaTech offers three health insurance plans through BlueCross BlueShield:

### Bronze Plan
- Monthly premium: $85/individual, $220/family
- Deductible: $3,000/individual, $6,000/family
- Out-of-pocket max: $7,000/individual, $14,000/family
- Covers 70% after deductible

### Silver Plan
- Monthly premium: $150/individual, $380/family
- Deductible: $1,500/individual, $3,000/family
- Out-of-pocket max: $5,000/individual, $10,000/family
- Covers 80% after deductible

### Gold Plan
- Monthly premium: $250/individual, $600/family
- Deductible: $500/individual, $1,000/family
- Out-of-pocket max: $3,000/individual, $6,000/family
- Covers 90% after deductible

NovaTech covers 80% of employee premiums and 50% of dependent premiums.

## Dental and Vision

- Dental: Delta Dental PPO included at no cost to employees. Covers two cleanings/year, basic procedures at 80%, major procedures at 50%.
- Vision: VSP plan included. Covers annual exam plus $200 frame/lens allowance or contact lens allowance.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

- Employees can contribute up to the IRS annual limit
- NovaTech matches 100% of the first 4% of salary contributed
- Vesting schedule: immediate for employee contributions, 3-year cliff vesting for employer match
- Eligible from first day of employment

## Life and Disability Insurance

- Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid, up to $500,000)
- Supplemental life: Available at group rates up to 5x salary
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 26 weeks, up to age 65

## Wellness Benefits

- $1,200/year wellness stipend (gym, fitness apps, mental health apps)
- Free access to Headspace meditation app
- Quarterly wellness challenges with prizes
- Annual biometric screenings (optional)
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 8 free counseling sessions/year
