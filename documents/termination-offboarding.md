# Termination & Offboarding Policy

## Voluntary Resignation

- Employees must provide minimum 2 weeks written notice (4 weeks for directors and above)
- Notice submitted to direct manager with copy to HR
- Final day is determined by the notice period unless otherwise agreed
- PTO cannot be used during the notice period without manager approval
- Exit interview conducted by HR on or before the final day

## Involuntary Termination

Grounds for immediate termination include:
- Theft or fraud
- Violence or threats of violence
- Gross negligence causing significant harm
- Violation of data security policies resulting in a breach
- Harassment after a prior written warning
- Working under the influence of drugs or alcohol

Non-immediate termination (with warning):
- Consistent underperformance after PIP completion
- Repeated policy violations
- Insubordination

## Severance

- Voluntary resignation: No severance
- Involuntary (non-cause): 2 weeks pay per year of service (minimum 4 weeks, maximum 26 weeks)
- Layoff/restructuring: 3 weeks pay per year of service + 3 months COBRA coverage
- Severance requires signing a separation agreement and release of claims

## Offboarding Checklist

On the employee's last day:
1. Return all company equipment (laptop, badge, peripherals)
2. Return any company credit cards
3. Transfer ownership of documents and projects
4. Remove personal files from company systems
5. Access revoked to all systems by end of business on final day
6. Final paycheck includes unused PTO balance
7. COBRA information sent within 14 days
8. 401(k) rollover instructions provided

## Post-Employment Obligations

- Non-compete: 12 months (where legally enforceable)
- Non-solicitation: 12 months (employees and customers)
- Confidentiality: Indefinite
- Return of intellectual property: Immediate upon termination
