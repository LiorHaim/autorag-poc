# Business Travel Policy

## Booking Procedures

All business travel must be booked through the NovaTech travel portal powered by Navan (formerly TripActions). Direct bookings are not reimbursable except in emergencies.

- Domestic travel: Book at least 14 days in advance
- International travel: Book at least 21 days in advance
- Last-minute travel (under 7 days): Requires director-level approval

## Air Travel

- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy for flights 6-10 hours
- Business class for flights over 10 hours, when required for medical accommodation, or for VP-level and above
- First class is not permitted without CEO approval
- Employees on international assignments exceeding 30 days may book business class regardless of flight duration
- Employees may keep frequent flyer miles for personal use
- Select the lowest reasonable fare within 2 hours of preferred departure time

## Ground Transportation

- Airport transfers: Ride-share (Uber/Lyft) or airport shuttle preferred
- Rental cars: Approved for trips where public transport is impractical or multiple site visits are required
- Always purchase rental insurance through the company portal (not at the counter)
- Personal vehicle: Reimbursed at $0.67/mile; requires pre-approval for trips over 100 miles

## Accommodations

- Book hotels rated 3-star or above
- Standard room unless medical accommodation requires otherwise
- Maximum nightly rates:
  - Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo): $400
  - Tier 2 cities (Chicago, Austin, Berlin, Sydney): $250
  - All other locations: $200
- Extended stays (5+ nights): Consider Airbnb or serviced apartments for cost savings

## Per Diem

When traveling domestically:
- Breakfast: $15
- Lunch: $25
- Dinner: $35
- Incidentals: $10/day

International per diem follows U.S. State Department rates for the destination city.

## Travel Safety

- Register all international trips with the security team
- Download the NovaTech Emergency app before traveling
- Keep copies of passport and travel documents in secure company storage
- Emergency assistance: +1-512-555-0922 (available 24/7)
