# Parental Leave Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have been with NovaTech for at least 6 months are eligible for parental leave. This applies to birth parents, adoptive parents, and foster parents.

## Leave Duration

- **Birth parents**: 16 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Non-birth parents**: 8 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Adoption/Foster**: 12 weeks of fully paid leave

Leave can be taken consecutively or split into two blocks within the first 12 months following birth/placement.

## Benefits During Leave

- All health, dental, and vision benefits continue during parental leave
- 401(k) matching continues based on pre-leave contribution rate
- PTO does not accrue during unpaid portions of extended leave
- Life and disability insurance remain active

## Return to Work

- Employees are guaranteed return to the same or equivalent position
- A phased return schedule is available: 60% schedule for the first 2 weeks back, 80% for weeks 3-4, full schedule from week 5
- Lactation rooms are available in all NovaTech offices
- Backup childcare benefit: 20 days/year of subsidized emergency childcare through Care.com

## Extended Leave

Employees may request up to 8 additional weeks of unpaid leave beyond the standard allocation. This request requires HR and VP approval. Extended leave does not guarantee the same position upon return, though NovaTech will make reasonable efforts to place the employee in a comparable role.
