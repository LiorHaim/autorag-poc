# Performance Review Process

## Review Cycle

NovaTech conducts performance reviews twice per year:
- **Mid-year review**: July (lightweight check-in, no rating)
- **Annual review**: January (full evaluation with rating and compensation review)

## Rating Scale

Performance is rated on a 5-point scale:
1. **Needs Improvement** - Does not meet expectations; improvement plan required
2. **Developing** - Partially meets expectations; growth areas identified
3. **Meets Expectations** - Consistently delivers quality work as expected
4. **Exceeds Expectations** - Frequently goes above and beyond requirements
5. **Exceptional** - Consistently delivers outstanding results with significant impact

## Review Components

Each review includes:
- **Self-assessment**: Employee rates themselves and provides examples
- **Manager assessment**: Manager provides ratings and detailed feedback
- **Peer feedback**: 3-5 peers provide 360-degree feedback (annual only)
- **Goal achievement**: Review of OKRs set at start of period
- **Development plan**: Learning goals and career growth plans for next period

## Compensation Adjustments

Annual compensation adjustments are tied to performance ratings:
- Rating 1-2: No increase; performance improvement plan initiated
- Rating 3: 3-5% merit increase
- Rating 4: 5-8% merit increase + bonus eligibility
- Rating 5: 8-12% merit increase + bonus + equity refresh eligibility

## Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs)

Employees rated "Needs Improvement" are placed on a 60-day PIP:
- Specific measurable goals are set
- Weekly check-ins with manager
- HR involvement throughout
- At end of 60 days: re-evaluation determines continued employment, PIP extension, or termination

## Promotion Criteria

Promotions require:
- Minimum 12 months in current role
- Rating of "Exceeds Expectations" or higher for the last 2 review cycles
- Demonstration of next-level competencies
- Manager nomination and skip-level approval
- Headcount availability in the target role
