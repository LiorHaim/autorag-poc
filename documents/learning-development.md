# Learning & Development Program

## Annual Learning Budget

Every full-time employee receives a $3,000 annual learning budget that can be used for:
- Conference attendance (including registration, travel, and accommodation)
- Online courses and certifications (Coursera, Udemy, cloud certifications, etc.)
- Books (physical or digital)
- Workshops and bootcamps
- Professional coaching (up to 6 sessions/year)

The learning budget resets on January 1 and does not carry over.

## Tuition Reimbursement

Employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible for tuition reimbursement:
- Up to $10,000/year for degree programs related to their role
- Must maintain a GPA of 3.0 or higher
- Requires manager and HR approval before enrollment
- Employee must remain with NovaTech for 2 years after reimbursement or repay prorated amount

## Internal Learning Resources

- **LinkedIn Learning**: Unlimited access for all employees
- **NovaTech Academy**: Internal courses on company products, processes, and leadership
- **Tech Talks**: Weekly 30-minute presentations by engineers (Fridays at 4 PM)
- **Book Club**: Monthly engineering and leadership book discussions
- **Mentorship Program**: 6-month structured mentoring relationships (apply quarterly)

## Certification Support

NovaTech covers 100% of certification exam fees for:
- AWS (all levels)
- Google Cloud (all levels)
- Kubernetes (CKA, CKAD, CKS)
- Terraform (Associate and Professional)
- Scrum/Agile certifications (CSM, PSM, SAFe)

If the employee fails the exam, NovaTech will cover one retake. Subsequent retakes are at the employee's expense.

## Career Paths

NovaTech offers two career tracks:
- **Individual Contributor (IC) Track**: Junior → Mid → Senior → Staff → Principal → Distinguished
- **Management Track**: Team Lead → Manager → Senior Manager → Director → VP → SVP

Employees can switch tracks with manager support and skip-level approval. Switching does not require a new role search if moving laterally.
