# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical and professional standards expected of all NovaTech employees, contractors, and partners. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## Professional Behavior

All employees must:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity
- Communicate professionally in all channels (email, Slack, meetings)
- Respect diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and working styles
- Avoid gossip, rumors, and negative talk about colleagues
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries

## Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any situation where personal interests could conflict with company interests, including:
- Financial interests in competitors, customers, or vendors
- Family members employed by competitors or vendors
- Outside employment or consulting that could affect NovaTech duties
- Serving on boards of companies related to NovaTech's business

Disclosures are made to HR via the ethics portal and reviewed within 10 business days.

## Anti-Harassment Policy

NovaTech has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind, including:
- Sexual harassment (unwelcome advances, requests for favors, verbal/physical conduct)
- Bullying or intimidation
- Discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any protected characteristic
- Retaliation against anyone who reports harassment

Report harassment to HR, your manager, your skip-level manager, or anonymously through the Ethics Hotline: 1-800-555-ETHX.

## Confidentiality

Employees must protect confidential information during and after employment:
- Do not share proprietary information with unauthorized parties
- Do not discuss confidential matters in public places
- Return all company materials upon termination
- Non-disclosure obligations survive employment termination

## Gifts and Entertainment

- Employees may accept gifts valued under $100
- Gifts over $100 must be disclosed to HR
- Government officials may never receive gifts of any value
- Cash or cash equivalents (gift cards) may never be accepted
