# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## General Guidelines

All business expenses must be pre-approved by the employee's manager for amounts exceeding $100. Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25.

## Expense Categories and Limits

### Travel
- **Airfare**: Economy class for flights under 6 hours; premium economy for flights 6-10 hours. Business class for flights over 10 hours requires VP approval.
- **Hotels**: Up to $250/night in standard markets; up to $400/night in high-cost cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo, Singapore)
- **Rental Cars**: Mid-size vehicles or below. SUVs require justification.
- **Mileage**: $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use on company business
- **Meals while traveling**: Up to $75/day (breakfast $15, lunch $25, dinner $35)

### Client Entertainment
- Up to $150 per person for client meals
- Events over $500 total require director-level approval
- All client entertainment must include a business purpose note

### Office Supplies
- Up to $50/month without approval
- Items over $50 require manager approval

### Professional Development
- Conference attendance: up to $3,000/year including travel
- Books and online courses: up to $500/year without approval

## Submission Process

1. Log into the expense portal at expenses.novatech.internal
2. Create a new expense report
3. Attach photos of all receipts
4. Categorize each expense
5. Add business justification
6. Submit for manager approval

Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of approval and deposited directly to the employee's bank account.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Alcohol (except at approved client entertainment events)
- Personal travel extensions
- Gym memberships (covered separately under wellness benefits)
- Traffic or parking violations
- First-class airfare without executive approval
