# Compensation Philosophy

## Market Positioning

NovaTech targets the 75th percentile of market compensation for all roles. We benchmark against:
- Companies of similar size (500-2000 employees)
- Same industry (enterprise SaaS)
- Same geographic market (adjusted for cost of living)

Compensation data is refreshed annually using Radford, Levels.fyi, and Pave surveys.

## Compensation Components

### Base Salary
- Reviewed annually during the January performance cycle
- Adjustments based on performance rating, market movement, and internal equity
- Off-cycle adjustments are possible for retention or promotion cases

### Annual Bonus
- Eligible employees: All full-time employees at Level 4 (Senior) and above
- Target bonus: 10-20% of base salary depending on level
- Based on: 50% company performance + 50% individual performance
- Paid in March for the prior year's performance

### Equity (Stock Options / RSUs)
- Granted at hire and during annual refresh cycles
- New hire grants vest over 4 years with 1-year cliff
- Refresh grants vest over 3 years, no cliff
- Equity refresh eligibility: Rating of "Exceeds Expectations" or higher

## Pay Transparency

NovaTech publishes salary bands for all roles internally:
- Each role has a defined band with minimum, midpoint, and maximum
- Employees can view their position within their band in Workday
- Salary bands are updated annually
- New hires typically start between minimum and midpoint

## Geographic Adjustments

Compensation is adjusted based on location:
- Tier 1 (SF, NYC, Seattle): 100% of band
- Tier 2 (Austin, Denver, Boston, LA): 90% of band
- Tier 3 (Other US metros): 85% of band
- Tier 4 (Rural US): 80% of band
- International: Based on local market data
