# Data Privacy Policy

## Overview

NovaTech is committed to protecting personal data in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations. This policy applies to all employees who handle personal data of customers, employees, or partners.

## Data Collection Principles

We collect personal data only when:
- There is a legitimate business purpose
- The individual has provided consent (where required)
- Collection is limited to what is necessary (data minimization)
- Data accuracy is maintained through regular updates

## Customer Data Handling

### Categories of Data We Collect
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage data (feature usage, login history)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored by NovaTech)
- Support interactions (tickets, chat transcripts)
- Analytics data (anonymized usage patterns)

### Data Retention
- Active customer data: Retained during subscription + 90 days
- Churned customer data: Deleted within 90 days of account closure
- Support tickets: Retained for 3 years for quality purposes
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years per financial regulations
- Analytics data: Retained indefinitely in anonymized form

## Employee Data

Employee personal data is managed by HR and includes:
- Contact information, emergency contacts
- Payroll and benefits information
- Performance records
- Employment history

Employee data is retained for 7 years after termination per legal requirements.

## Data Subject Rights

Individuals may exercise the following rights:
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of their personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Portability**: Receive data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object**: Opt out of certain processing activities

Requests are submitted to privacy@novatech.internal and must be fulfilled within 30 days.

## Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach:
1. Security team contains the breach within 1 hour
2. Impact assessment completed within 24 hours
3. Affected individuals notified within 72 hours
4. Regulatory authorities notified within 72 hours (where required)
5. Post-incident review completed within 14 days
