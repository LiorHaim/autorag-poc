# Workplace Safety & Facilities

## Office Hours and Access

- Offices are open Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM
- After-hours access requires badge entry and is logged
- Weekend access requires manager pre-approval via facilities portal
- Visitors must sign in at reception and be escorted at all times

## Emergency Procedures

### Fire
- Evacuate immediately using nearest exit (maps posted at all exits)
- Do not use elevators
- Assembly point: parking lot northwest corner (Austin), Hyde Park corner (London)
- Floor wardens will sweep their zones
- Do not re-enter until "all clear" announcement

### Medical Emergency
- Call 911 (US) or 999 (UK) immediately
- Notify reception/security
- AED devices located: break rooms, main lobby, gym
- First aid kits located in every break room and at reception

### Severe Weather
- Move to interior rooms away from windows
- Austin office: shelter in basement level B1
- Monitor official communications via Slack #emergency channel

## Ergonomics

- All employees receive an ergonomic assessment within first 30 days
- Standing desks available upon request at no cost
- Ergonomic accessories (keyboard trays, monitor arms, footrests) provided through IT portal
- Report discomfort or pain to HR for workstation adjustment

## Sustainability

NovaTech is committed to environmental sustainability:
- All offices use 100% renewable energy
- Single-use plastics eliminated from all offices
- Composting and recycling stations on every floor
- Electric vehicle charging stations at Austin and London offices (free for employees)
- Carbon offset program for all business travel
