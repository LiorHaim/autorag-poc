# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible for remote work. Remote work arrangements must be approved by the employee's direct manager and HR.

## Hybrid Schedule

NovaTech operates on a hybrid model:
- **Minimum 2 days per week in-office** for employees within 50 miles of an office
- **Fully remote** available for employees beyond 50 miles from any NovaTech office
- Tuesday and Thursday are designated "anchor days" when teams are expected in-office

## Remote Work Equipment

NovaTech provides the following for remote workers:
- Laptop (refreshed every 3 years)
- External monitor (up to 27")
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset with microphone
- $500 one-time home office stipend for desk, chair, or other ergonomic equipment

## Internet and Utilities

Employees working remotely receive a monthly stipend of $75 to offset internet and utility costs. This is paid automatically with the regular paycheck and is taxable income.

## Security Requirements

Remote workers must:
- Use the company VPN for all work-related network access
- Enable full-disk encryption on all devices
- Lock screens when stepping away
- Not use public Wi-Fi without VPN
- Report any security incidents within 1 hour to security@novatech.internal

## Communication Expectations

Remote employees must:
- Be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during business hours
- Keep their calendar updated with availability
- Turn on video during team meetings unless bandwidth issues occur
