# NovaTech Solutions Employee Handbook

## Welcome to NovaTech Solutions

Welcome to NovaTech Solutions! Founded in 2018, NovaTech is a leading provider of cloud-native enterprise software. We are headquartered in Austin, Texas with offices in London, Berlin, and Singapore. Our mission is to empower organizations through intelligent automation. As of 2024, NovaTech employs over 1,200 people across four continents and serves more than 800 enterprise customers worldwide.

## Company Values

1. **Innovation First** - We encourage experimentation and creative problem-solving
2. **Customer Obsession** - Every decision starts with the customer
3. **Radical Transparency** - We share information openly across the organization
4. **Inclusive Excellence** - Diverse perspectives drive better outcomes
5. **Ownership Mentality** - Every employee is an owner of their work and outcomes

## Organizational Structure

NovaTech is organized into the following divisions:
- **Engineering**: Platform, Product, Infrastructure, and Security teams
- **Product**: Product Management, Design, and Research
- **Go-to-Market**: Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, and Partnerships
- **Operations**: Finance, Legal, HR, IT, and Facilities
- **Office of the CTO**: Architecture, Developer Relations, and Innovation Lab

Each division is led by a Vice President who reports to the CEO. Teams within divisions are organized into squads of 5-8 people, each with a Tech Lead or Team Lead.

## Employment Classifications

- **Full-time**: Employees working 40 hours per week with full benefits
- **Part-time**: Employees working 20-30 hours per week with prorated benefits
- **Contract**: Fixed-term engagements with specific deliverables
- **Intern**: Temporary learning-focused positions (12-16 weeks)

NovaTech is an at-will employer. This means that either the company or the employee may end the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice, subject to applicable law and any contractual obligations.

## Work Schedule

Standard business hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM local time, Monday through Friday. NovaTech operates on a flexible schedule policy - employees may adjust their start time between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM with manager approval. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM when all team members should be available.

Overtime for non-exempt employees is paid at 1.5x the regular hourly rate for hours exceeding 40 per week. Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager. Exempt employees are not eligible for overtime pay.

## Probationary Period

All new employees undergo a 90-day probationary period. During this time, performance is reviewed at 30, 60, and 90 days. Benefits eligibility begins on the first day of employment, but some benefits (such as tuition reimbursement) require completion of the probationary period.

## Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

NovaTech is committed to building an inclusive workplace where every employee feels they belong. Our DEI program includes:

- **Employee Resource Groups (ERGs)**: NovaTech sponsors 8 ERGs including Women in Tech, Black@NovaTech, Pride@NovaTech, Veterans@NovaTech, and others. Each ERG receives an annual budget of $10,000 for events and programs.
- **Inclusive Hiring**: All job postings are reviewed for inclusive language. Interview panels must include at least one member from an underrepresented group. We partner with organizations like Code2040, Out in Tech, and the National Society of Black Engineers.
- **Pay Equity Audits**: Conducted annually by an independent third party. Any unjustified gaps are corrected within 60 days.
- **Unconscious Bias Training**: Required for all employees annually, with additional training for hiring managers and people leaders.
- **Accommodation**: NovaTech provides reasonable accommodations for employees with disabilities. Requests are handled confidentially through HR and typically processed within 5 business days.

## Workplace Conduct and Anti-Harassment

NovaTech prohibits all forms of harassment, discrimination, and retaliation based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other legally protected characteristic.

Harassment includes but is not limited to: unwelcome comments or jokes, offensive images or materials, intimidation or bullying, unwanted physical contact, and quid pro quo requests. All employees are required to complete anti-harassment training within 30 days of hire and annually thereafter.

## Grievance and Complaint Procedures

Employees who experience or witness policy violations have multiple reporting channels:

1. **Direct Manager**: For workplace concerns that can be addressed at the team level
2. **HR Business Partner**: For formal complaints or when the direct manager is involved
3. **Ethics Hotline**: Anonymous reporting at 1-800-555-NOVA (6682) or ethics.novatech.internal
4. **Legal Department**: For concerns involving potential legal violations

All complaints are investigated within 10 business days. NovaTech strictly prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith. Investigation outcomes are communicated to the reporting party within 5 business days of completion, to the extent permitted by confidentiality obligations.

## ADA Accommodations

NovaTech complies with the Americans with Disabilities Act and provides reasonable accommodations for qualified individuals. The interactive accommodation process works as follows:

1. Employee submits an accommodation request to HR (form available at hr.novatech.internal/accommodations)
2. HR schedules a confidential discussion within 3 business days
3. Medical documentation may be requested if the disability is not obvious
4. HR, the employee, and the manager collaborate on potential accommodations
5. A decision is communicated within 10 business days of receiving all required documentation
6. Accommodations are reviewed annually or when circumstances change

Common accommodations include ergonomic equipment, modified work schedules, assistive technology, service animal approval, and remote work arrangements.

## Intellectual Property

All work product created by employees within the scope of their employment is the property of NovaTech Solutions. This includes inventions, code, designs, written materials, and other intellectual property. Employees must disclose any inventions made during employment. NovaTech does not claim ownership of inventions made entirely on the employee's own time using their own resources, provided they are unrelated to NovaTech's current or anticipated business.

## Policy Updates

This handbook is reviewed and updated annually. Material changes are communicated via company-wide email and posted to the HR portal at hr.novatech.internal. Employees are responsible for reviewing updates within 30 days of notification.

For questions about any policy in this handbook, contact your HR Business Partner or email hr@novatech.internal.
